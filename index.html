Spatial Analyst / GIS / Remote Sensing

Hello, my name is Habib Maskur Syihab, but you can call me Habib.

I am a undergraduate student at State University of Malang majoring in Geography. I have been working as GIS Staff at Hirfi Studio and GIS Local Assistant at USAID Adaptasi Perubahan Iklim (APIK) Regional Jawa Timur.

I am passionate about Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System (RS-GIS), especially when it comes to data for greater good. Besides spatial data analytics, currently I am learning spatial data science (with Python and R) and web development. You can see my portfolio here, my resume on LinkedIn, or my other writings on Medium (coming soon).
